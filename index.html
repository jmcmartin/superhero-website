Mister Fantastic have many interesting powers that are individual to him including the following:

  1. Power to stretch his body into any shape
  2. Invincibility
  3. Above average intelligence
  4. Leadership skills and qualities to lead the Fantastic 4

Throughout his career, Mister Fantastic has been recognized for many feats including:

  • Defeating villans such as Galactus and the Silver Surfer
  • Has several degrees of science including chemistry, electrical engineering, and physics
  • Leader of the Fantastic 4
  • Voyage through the Van Allen belt

Mister Fantastic has experience in the work field due to his occupations in the fields of mechanical, aerospace and electrical engineering, chemistry, all levels of physics, and human and alien biology. He also knows the responsibilities of being in a higher up leadership position.
